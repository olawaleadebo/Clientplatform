## â“ FAQ

**Q: What makes these "special"?**  
A: They're assigned for specific purposes and usually require priority attention.

**Q: Can I see these in my regular client list?**  
A: No - they only appear in the Special Numbers tab to keep things organized.

**Q: What if I can't complete the call right away?**  
A: That's okay! The number stays in your pending list until you mark it complete.

**Q: Can I call the same number twice?**  
A: Yes, until you mark it complete. Once completed, it's archived.

**Q: Where do completed calls go?**  
A: They're archived. Your manager can see the archive if needed.

**Q: Can I uncomplete a call?**  
A: No - once completed, it's permanent. Contact your manager if you need to re-engage.
